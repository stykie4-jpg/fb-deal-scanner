Be blunt and direct — Aidan doesn't want fluff or long explanations.

**Why:** He's running a business and values speed. He said "Lock in nothing happening" when things weren't working — wants fast results.

**How to apply:** Short responses. Lead with what's happening or what changed. Skip lengthy analysis unless asked.

When code is broken, fix it and restart — don't explain theory first.
