Aidan is a DC/VA/MD bike reseller. The business is not just "buy low, sell high on bikes." Three pillars matter for any deal recommendation:

**1. Trade chains are central.** Roughly half his deals are trades, not cash buys. Documented examples:
- Upgraded Ultra Bee → Sur Ron LBX + Razor Stage 2 M2 combo ($4k value)
- DRZ400 ($2,900 basis) → R6 + $800 cash back ($2,100 net basis on R6)

**Why:** sellers often won't drop their cash price but will trade for inventory. Aidan can offer current bikes in lieu of cash.

**How to apply:** listings that say "open to trades" / "will trade" should score higher than the cash-only math suggests. Always mention the trade angle in negotiation tips. Reference current inventory when relevant.

**2. Parts arbitrage works.** Proven: bought a Sur Ron Light Bee battery alone for $100, sold for $600 (6x). Standalone ebike batteries / motors / controllers / premium forks listed cheap = flip targets, not noise.

**3. Asset categories he actually flips** (treat the business as "anything with margin," not just bikes):
- Electric mini bikes (Sur Ron, Talaria, Razor) — bread and butter
- Dirt bikes (KTM, CRF, KX, YZ, Husky, Beta — 250/450)
- Sport bikes (R6, etc.)
- **Vehicles** — old work trucks proven (1990 Chevy $2k → $3.8k). Single-cab pre-2000 pickups under $3k that run are flips.
- **Parts** — Sur Ron / Talaria batteries, motors, controllers, premium forks — see pillar #2
- **Power tools** (DeWalt, Milwaukee Fuel, Makita), **outdoor power equipment** (snowblowers off-season, generators, pressure washers), **trailers**, **watercraft** April-Sept (jet skis, kayaks, paddleboards), **UTVs / side-by-sides**, regular bicycles (Trek/Specialized/Santa Cruz), opportunistic high-margin home goods (KitchenAid, Yeti, Big Green Egg, Traeger)

**Seasonality is real in DMV.** Buy off-season, sell in-season. Snowblowers cheap in summer / premium Oct-Feb. Bikes & watercraft opposite. Trucks & tools are year-round steady.

**Best score on record:** 2025 Sur Ron LBX bought $1,600 → sold $3,700 (+$2,100). Use as the high-water comp.
